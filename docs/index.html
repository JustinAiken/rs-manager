rs-manager

Stats Manager for Rocksmith Remastered 2014

Rocksmith is great but its UI leaves a lot to be desired especially when you have a lot of songs in your collection. This app uses Rocksmith + Steam data to consolidate stats, mastery, dlc and song lists under one roof.

Features

Stats

Track your high level stats ( overall mastery, completion and score attack ) of your entire collection and setlists. You can also drill down and see stats on a per song basis ( mastery, progress history, fc status ).

Steam

Link your steam account and enhance your dlc searching and buying experience. rs-manager tracks your purchase stats and allows you to, among other things, sort your dlc's by purchase date and discover new dlc's based on genre!

Setlists

Create unlimited setlists to sort and categorize your collection. You can create setlists and add songs manually or via filters. Filters are super powerful, since you can combine them in any order you like to create a nuanced collection. for eg: you can create setlists with only Eb standard songs that dont use capo, which you havent played a lot and has a score attack master badge of gold or below!

Export to Rocksmith

Export any setlist created with
rs-manager to your rocksmith in-game list ( integrates rsrtools ).

Realtime Stats

Track note hits/misses and perfects in realtime in the Rocksmith Live mode ( integrates RockSniffer ). It also lets you record the raw audio of your playing!

PSARC Explorer

Explore and extract files from rocksmith psarc files (dlc). rs-manager can also import new psarcs downloaded from steam automatically while the app is running.
